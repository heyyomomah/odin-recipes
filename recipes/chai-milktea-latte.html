Chai Milk Tea Latte

Discreption

A chai milk tea latte, or simply a chai latte, is a spiced black tea drink made with steamed milk, often topped with a layer of milk foam, and is similar to a masala chai but with more milk and a milder tea flavor.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup boiling water
  • 1 chai tea bag
  • 1 ½ teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • ¾ cup milk

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Pour boiling water over chai tea bag in a mug; let steep 4 to 6 minutes. Remove and discard tea bag.
  3. Stir honey and sugar into tea to dissolve. Stir milk through tea to serve.
